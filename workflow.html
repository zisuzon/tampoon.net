Workflow to make changes on the FO (front end) part

A) Before any modification open SmartGit and "pull" any modifications someone else might have done meanwhile
B) Open phpstorm and the tampoon project
C) Make your modifications (no need to save)
D) Open SmartGit and hit "cmd + k"
E) Enter a commit message then click on "commit & push"
D) Wait around 20 seconds to see the changes
